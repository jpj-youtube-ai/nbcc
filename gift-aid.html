Gift Aid

Add Gift Aid to your donation

If you are a UK taxpayer, the Night Before Christmas Campaign can reclaim the tax on your donation, adding a quarter to its value at no extra cost to you. Complete the short declaration below and NBCC will do the rest.

Your Gift Aid declaration

We ask for these so HMRC can match your donation to your tax record.

Gift Aid depends on paying UK Income Tax or Capital Gains Tax, not on where you live. You can still Gift Aid from an overseas address if you are a UK taxpayer.

I want to Gift Aid my donation to the Night Before Christmas Campaign. I am a UK taxpayer and understand that if I pay less Income Tax and/or Capital Gains Tax than the amount of Gift Aid claimed on all my donations in that tax year it is my responsibility to pay any difference.

Questions about your donation? Call 01292 811 015 or email giving@nbcc.scot.
