How can we use your story? *

Since you are happy for us to share your story publicly, would you also let us include either of these details.

We keep stories as part of our permanent archive, to show the difference NBCC makes over time, unless you ask us to withdraw or delete yours. Withdrawing means we stop using your story and mark it withdrawn; deleting means we erase your details for good. You can ask us to do either at any time by emailing info@nbcc.scot or using our contact form.
