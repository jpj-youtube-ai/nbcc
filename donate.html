We ask for these only if you add Gift Aid, so HMRC can match your donation to your tax record.

Which donations does this declaration cover?

Gift Aid depends on paying UK Income Tax or Capital Gains Tax, not on where you live. You can still Gift Aid from an overseas address if you are a UK taxpayer.

Each partner's Gift Aid declaration

A thank you for monthly donors

What monthly donors receive

Give monthly and you help NBCC bring comfort, dignity and joy to children, young people and vulnerable adults across South West Scotland all year round. Here is how we say thank you.

All monthly donors

  • Your name, or your business name, added to our Donors Page, unless you choose to remain anonymous.
  • Our donor newsletter, keeping you in the loop on the difference your support helps make.

Platinum donors also receive

  • A special thank you on our social media channels, celebrating your generous ongoing support.
  • An optional digital supporter badge for your website, ideal for businesses.
  • A personalised supporter certificate, printed and posted, or sent digitally.
Peace of mind

Giving with confidence

Setting up a donation to NBCC is simple, secure and entirely in your control. If you ever need a hand, a real person is here to help.

Cancel any time

Monthly donations can be changed or cancelled whenever you like. Direct Debits are protected by the Direct Debit Guarantee, and we tell you the amount and the date in advance, before the first collection and before any change.

Secure and simple

Donations are handled securely by Stripe. Monthly giving should be set up by adults aged 18 or over.
