Gift Aid claims

NBCC reclaims 25p of tax from HMRC for every £1 given by a UK taxpayer who made a Gift Aid declaration. You file the claim through HMRC’s Charities Online service. This page prepares the claim and tracks it; it does not file it for you.

How a Gift Aid claim works, step by step
  1. One-time setup (HMRC). Enrol for Charities Online in NBCC’s HMRC online account (choose “Charities”). HMRC posts an activation code; it arrives in about a week and must be used within 28 days.
  2. Build a batch here. In 1. Ready to claim below, tick the Gift-Aided gifts you want to claim and add them to a batch. One batch is one HMRC claim (up to 1,000 donations; above that HMRC needs claiming software).
  3. Export the batch. Under 2. Claim batches, click Export CSV. The file lists each gift with the donor’s name, house name/number, postcode, date and amount: exactly the columns HMRC’s schedule needs.
  4. Fill HMRC’s schedule spreadsheet. Download HMRC’s official Gift Aid schedule spreadsheet (an .ods file) and copy the exported rows into it. HMRC only accepts their spreadsheet, so don’t rename its worksheet tab or it won’t attach.
  5. Submit on Charities Online. Log in to HMRC, attach the filled schedule to the online claim form, and send it.
  6. Mark the batch submitted. Come back here and click Submit on the batch, so NBCC’s records match what you sent HMRC.
  7. HMRC pays. Repayment arrives by BACS, normally within about 4 weeks (HMRC target: 20 working days).
  8. Keep the records. Keep each donor’s declaration for 6 years. NBCC stores these automatically so HMRC can audit them.

Refunded a gift you had already claimed? See 3. Adjustment due. Enter that amount in the adjustment box on your next Charities Online claim, which repays HMRC by reducing the new claim. Deadline: claim within 4 years of the end of the tax year the gift was received. This is a plain-English summary of HMRC guidance, not tax advice.

GASDS deadline

GASDS (the Gift Aid Small Donations Scheme) tops up small cash and contactless gifts, but the claim window is only two years from the end of the tax year of collection. These small gifts are approaching or past that cliff and are not yet marked as claimed. Tick the ones you have claimed under GASDS to clear them.
