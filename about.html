About us

Powered by kindness, driven by community

The Night Before Christmas Campaign (NBCC) is a volunteer run charity in Annbank, Ayrshire, supporting children, young people and vulnerable adults across South West Scotland, from Girvan to Largs.

Our story

“Do all children get a Christmas Eve box like I do?”

Tygan, age twelve, Annbank, 2015

That single question, asked of her mum Jodie (our Head Elf) and her gran Isabella, became the Night Before Christmas Campaign. The answer was no, not every child wakes up to the magic of Christmas, and the three of them set out to change that.

In their first year they delivered 90 boxes filled with love and gifts. Seeing people sleeping rough on a cold morning in Glasgow pushed them to do more, and to register as a charity. From 593 bags to more than 7,000, the campaign has grown every year, and so has the need.

The story continues, and you can be part of it.
