Known at Christmas

Red Bags Full of Joy

Our signature programme: thoughtful, personalised gifts that bring dignity, comfort and moments of joy to people who might otherwise go without at Christmas.

Each bag gives support around the value of £50, and in 2025, 7,657 of them went out across South West Scotland.

Why your donation matters

Every pound helps remind someone that they have not been forgotten.

Your donation helps NBCC provide thoughtful gifts, essential support and moments of comfort, dignity and joy for people who may otherwise go without at Christmas.

Donations also help cover the essential costs of running the charity, so we can keep reaching those who need us year after year.
