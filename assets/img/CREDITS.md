# Image credits & consent manifest

Tracks every image in `assets/img/`, its source, and its **consent status**.
Consent rule: **no beneficiary or volunteer photograph ships without recorded,
informed consent.** Beneficiary imagery (children, young people and vulnerable
adults) additionally requires guardian/safeguarding consent. Until consent is on
file, the slot uses a clearly-labelled placeholder.
